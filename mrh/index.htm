Accepting elacoin

  1. Download a elacoin client
  2. When a customer wants to buy something, send them a elacoin address where their payment should be sent.
    • You can do this by clicking "New.." next to your address in the elacoin client and sending that address to the customer.
  3. When payment comes in to that address, send the goods to your customer. Depending on the value of what you're selling, you may wish to wait until the payment shows Confirmed.
  4. To issue a refund, obtain from the customer the elacoin address where the refund payment should be sent. The refund address will likely be different from the address used when the customer sent payment, especially if an EWallet or exchange was used by the customer.
  5. Download an elacoin image from here to display on your website. Images are Free to redistribute. Right click and select "save as" on the image(s) you would like to display on your site.
